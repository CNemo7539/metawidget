Play with Metawidget right now, in your browser! Click the button below to open a Groovy console (may take a few minutes to download initially) and populate it with a small script that:

  • defines a business model
  • configures inspectors for Groovy, JPA and Hibernate Validator
  • launches a Swing-based Metawiget

You can then play by:

  • running the code
  • changing the business model by adding fields, altering the annotations etc.
  • importing your own business object JARs to see how Metawidget renders them

If you like what you see, download the full distribution which includes data binding support, pluggable layouts, multiple inspectors, Web-based and mobile-based Metawidgets and more!
