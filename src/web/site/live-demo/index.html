Play with Metawidget right now, in your browser!

Click the button below to start a Groovy console (may take a few minutes to download initially) and populate it with a small script that:

  • defines a business model
  • configures inspectors for Groovy, JPA and Hibernate Validator
  • launches a Swing-based Metawiget

You can then play with Metawidget by:

  • running the code
  • changing the business model by adding fields, altering annotations etc.
  • importing your own business object JARs to see how Metawidget renders them
